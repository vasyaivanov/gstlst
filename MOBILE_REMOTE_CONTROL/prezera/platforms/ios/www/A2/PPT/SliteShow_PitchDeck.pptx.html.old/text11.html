Competition/Differentiators

Differentiators:

  • Every feature we just showed! (except for sharing)

  • Not just set of features, but new platform

Competition:

  • Powerpoint (95% of presentation market)

    • There is a huge movement who to switch from PPT to anything
    • Can do none of what we showed.
    • Old, ancient technology designed in 1990
    • Cost money and does not work on any platform

  • Google Drive Presentations

    • Can do none of what we showed (except for sharing)
    • Good for creating, very bad for presenting

  • Slideshare

    • Can do none of what we showed (except for sharing)
    • Has all and more limitations of a Powerpoint slide
    • Good for sharing, bad for creating

  • Other competition: Prezo, MyPoint, i-Clickr

    • Can do none of what we showed (except for sharing)
    • Have all traditional limitation of Powerpoint
